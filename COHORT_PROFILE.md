# Cohort Profile -- Pancreatic Cancer

**Cohort ID:** TCGA PAAD
**Data Source:** TCGA, accessed via UCSC Xena Browser (xenabrowser.net)
**Primary Endpoint:** Overall Survival (OS)
**Samples:** 183
**Event Rate:** 52%

## Clinical Context

Pancreatic ductal adenocarcinoma -- one of the most lethal solid tumors with median survival under 12 months. Strong BPF performance in a high-mortality cancer with urgent clinical need.
